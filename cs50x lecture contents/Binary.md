#CountingSystem

The Binary, or base-2, system is different of the [[Unary]] one, being a posicional, not an additive, [[counting system]].

Using 2 symbols to represent numbers that could be anything, but in [[Computer Science]] it is zeros and ones, physically zero (0) it's like a turned off "bulbs" and one (1) a turned on one, they are 2 binary digits/2 [[bit]]s.
But these "bulbs" are smaller, called [[transistors]], tiny light switches that can be turned on and off for data storing.
	For instance, with only 3 "bulbs" you could have 8 different patters instead of only 3 as the [[Unary]] system.
